How can we help you?

Browse our frequently asked questions or search for a specific topic.

Buying

Click on the Register/Sign in button at the top right, submit your company details, uploading GST and PAN documents for verification. Once approved by our team, you will receive full bidding and buying privileges.

Yes, all Direct Sell and certified auction vehicles undergo a rigorous 150-point inspection process by our qualified engineers. The full inspection report, including structural integrity and engine diagnostics, is visible on the vehicle listing page.

Once payment is cleared and RC transfer is initiated, you can opt for AutoHub logistics delivery to your preferred location or schedule a self-pickup from the vehicle's current yard.

Selling

Our AI valuation engine takes inputs like manufacturing year, kilometers driven, ownership history, and overall condition. It then cross-references this data with thousands of current market transactions to estimate a fair base range.

If a vehicle does not meet its reserve price, it is marked as "Unsold." You will have the option to lower the reserve and re-run it in the next auction event, or list it directly on the marketplace for interested buyers.

Auctions

Yes. While any registered user can view live auctions, you must have an active "Pro Buyer" or "Enterprise" subscription tier to place active bids on institutional events.

You can enter your maximum allowed bid, and our system will automatically place bids on your behalf at the minimum increment necessary to keep you in the lead, up to your maximum limit.
