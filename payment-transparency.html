How Money Flows on AutoHub

Understanding our secure, dealer-verified payment ecosystem.

1. Buyer Purchase

You explore the marketplace, select commercial vehicles, and finalize the purchase order online.

2. Dealer Generates Invoice

The supplier dealer validates the order and structures a formal tax invoice mapped directly to your PO.

3. Payment Execution (Offline)

You process the payment via Bank Transfer, RTGS, or Cheque directly to the Dealer's registered bank account.

4. Dealer Records Payment

Upon receiving the funds in their bank account, the dealer manually logs the receipt into AutoHub.

5. Transaction Settled

AutoHub automatically allocates the recorded payment to the invoice, calculates outstanding balances, and finalizes the ledger.
